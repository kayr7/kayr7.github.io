This is the first post on the rebuilt version of kay-rottmann.de.

## Why a rebuild

The old site had grown stale, and I wanted something that:

- loads fast on any device
- is easy to update by just writing Markdown
- respects my visitors' privacy by default - while also enabling me to use analytics which I can use in my lectures


## Stack

The new site is built with [Astro](https://astro.build), styled with Tailwind,
and uses [Klaro](https://klaro.kiprotect.com) for GDPR-compliant cookie consent
with Google Consent Mode v2.
